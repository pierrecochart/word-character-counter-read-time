How to Use

  • Input: Paste or type your text into the main editor.
  • Filters: Use the toggles to include/exclude numbers and punctuation from counts.
  • Speed: Adjust reading speed to see how long your content takes to consume for different audiences.
  • Insights: Check the "Deep Data" and "Top Letters" charts below for detailed text analysis.

This tool is entirely free and runs locally in your browser. If it helped you out, consider supporting future development!
